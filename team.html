Director & Lead Scientist

The Principal Investigator

Dr. Shrikant Tangade — Belagavi, India
Director, autoMoTIVe-X Lab
Professor, AMBIT, India · MSCA SoE Fellow, Inria, France
Expertise: Applied Cryptography and Automotive Cybersecurity

Dr. Tangade is a Professor at AMBIT and an MSCA Seal of Excellence Fellow at Inria Lille. With over a decade of research in V2X security and hybrid cryptography, he founded the autoMoTIVe-X Lab to bridge the gap between theoretical math and industrial-grade automotive security. His work focuses on stateless authentication and hardware-anchored resilience for the SDV era.
